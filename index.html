About

Salwa Dahlek is an artist whose practice began in her college years, with a few exhibitions to her name. Work and family life then took priority, and painting went on pause until she returned to it during the COVID lockdown. Living with chronic back pain, she rediscovered watercolour both as a creative outlet and a form of personal relief.

Her work emphasizes clarity, balance, and subtlety. Through delicate washes and considered compositions, Salwa explores stillness, light, and the quiet interplay of colour and form, creating works that invite reflection and calm observation.
